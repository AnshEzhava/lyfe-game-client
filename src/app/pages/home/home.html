How the Market Works

Prices are determined algorithmically, not by order books.

Constant-Product AMM

Buy Cost = B × s ⁄ (S − s)

Each stock has a liquidity pool of Branks (B) and shares (S). Buying s shares costs more as the pool depletes — large trades move the price.

The Project

About LyfeGame

LyfeGame is a personal project exploring how interconnected game systems create emergent economic behavior. The AMM-based stock exchange mirrors mechanics from decentralized finance, while the career and education systems gate progression through skill investment rather than time alone.

The AI news engine creates a feedback loop — market conditions influence the news, and the news influences the market — producing organic price movements without manual intervention.
